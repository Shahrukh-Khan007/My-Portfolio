About Me

Hello! My name is Shahrukh Khan. I am currently working as an Electrician at Pakistan Airports Authority (PAA). My specialization is in runway lighting systems and airfield lighting systems, which play a critical role in ensuring safe aircraft takeoff, landing, and airport operations.

Alongside my professional career in aviation electrical systems, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering at Muhammad Ali Jinnah University. My studies have helped me build a strong foundation in programming, web development, and software engineering principles.

I am passionate about technology and continuous learning. My goal is to combine my practical engineering experience with software development skills to create efficient, innovative, and impactful technological solutions.
