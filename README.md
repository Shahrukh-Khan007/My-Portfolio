# My-Portfolio
I am Shahrukh Khan, an Electrician at Pakistan Airports Authority (PAA) and an undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering at Muhammad Ali Jinnah University. My professional experience in the aviation sector has provided me with hands-on knowledge of critical electrical systems, particularly runway lighting systems and airfield lighting systems that support safe aircraft operations.

Throughout my career, I have gained practical experience in High Tension (HT) and Low Tension (LT) electrical systems, troubleshooting series circuits used in airfield lighting, and performing maintenance tasks at heights while following professional safety standards. This experience has strengthened my technical problem-solving skills, discipline, and ability to work in demanding environments.

Alongside my professional journey, I am developing my expertise in software engineering, with a strong interest in web development and technology. My portfolio includes projects such as “Gift For Pakistan,” an online gift delivery website designed to help people living within Pakistan or abroad send gifts to their loved ones on special occasions.

My educational journey began at Gulshan-e-Iqbal Secondary School, where I completed my primary and secondary education, followed by intermediate studies at Government Degree Boys College Gulzar-e-Hijri. I am currently continuing my academic growth at Muhammad Ali Jinnah University as a BS Software Engineering student.

This portfolio represents my journey of combining practical engineering experience with modern software development skills. It showcases my education, professional experience, technical abilities, projects, and passion for continuous learning and innovation.
