Gift For Pakistan - Online Gift Delivery Website

Gift For Pakistan is an online gift delivery website designed to connect people with their loved ones. Whether someone is living inside Pakistan or abroad, they can choose gifts for special occasions and we arrange the delivery to their family and friends.

This project was developed using front-end web technologies and helped me improve my skills in website design, user interface creation, and responsive web development.

Aviation Electrical & Airfield Lighting Experience

As an Electrician at Pakistan Airports Authority, I have practical experience working on High Tension (HT) and Low Tension (LT) electrical systems.

My specialized work includes maintaining and troubleshooting series circuits used in runway lighting systems and other airfield lighting systems, which are essential for safe aircraft operations.

I also have experience working at heights during maintenance and installation activities while following professional safety procedures.
